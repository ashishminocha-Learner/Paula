Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 15, 2025  ·  Last updated: April 15, 2025

The short version Your recordings and transcripts stay on your device. PAULA does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. When you choose to use third-party AI APIs (Whisper, Claude, Groq, Gemini), your content is sent directly to those providers under their own privacy terms — PAULA never sees it.

1. Who We Are

PAULA is an iOS application developed and operated by Ashish Minocha ("we", "us", or "our"). If you have questions about this policy, contact us at 93minash@gmail.com.

2. Data We Collect

PAULA is designed to collect as little data as possible.

2.1 Data stored only on your device

  • Audio recordings — saved locally in your app's private container. Never uploaded by PAULA.
  • Transcripts and summaries — generated and stored on-device using SwiftData.
  • App preferences — settings such as your chosen AI provider, stored in UserDefaults on-device.
  • API keys — stored exclusively in the iOS Keychain on your device. Never transmitted to PAULA's servers (we have no servers).

2.2 Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Third-Party AI Services

PAULA optionally connects to third-party AI APIs that you configure with your own API key. When you use these services, your audio or transcript is sent directly from your device to the respective provider. PAULA does not act as an intermediary and never stores this data.

Service What is sent Privacy policy
OpenAI Whisper API Audio file (when Whisper transcription is enabled) openai.com
Anthropic Claude Transcript text (for summarization) anthropic.com
Groq Transcript text (for summarization) groq.com
Google Gemini Transcript text (for summarization) google.com
Apple Speech Recognition Audio (on-device; may be sent to Apple for model improvement if you consent) apple.com
Your responsibility You are responsible for reviewing and accepting the privacy policies of any third-party service whose API key you add to PAULA. We recommend not recording confidential conversations using cloud-based AI services unless you have reviewed their data handling practices.

4. International Data Transfers

PAULA itself does not transfer your data internationally — all recordings and transcripts remain on your device. However, if you choose to use third-party AI APIs, your audio or transcript will be sent to servers operated by those providers, which are primarily located in the United States.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with data transfer restrictions, please be aware that these providers rely on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved mechanisms to lawfully transfer data from your region to the United States. Links to each provider's data transfer documentation are available in their respective privacy policies (see Section 3).

By adding an API key and enabling a third-party service in PAULA's Settings, you acknowledge and consent to your data being processed in the United States under that provider's terms.

5. Permissions We Request

Permission Why
Microphone Required to record audio. Recording only begins when you tap the Record button.
Speech Recognition Used for on-device transcription via Apple's Speech framework. Optional — you can use Whisper API instead.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

All recordings, transcripts, and summaries are stored locally on your device. You can delete individual recordings at any time from the Library screen, or delete everything at once via Settings → Delete All Recordings. Uninstalling PAULA removes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

PAULA is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has used PAULA in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or store your personal data on any server, there is no data held by us to access, correct, or delete. All data is on your device and entirely under your control.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you have rights under GDPR. If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA. In both cases, the relevant data is stored exclusively on your own device — please use the in-app deletion tools to exercise those rights.

8. Security

API keys are stored in the iOS Keychain with kSecAttrAccessibleWhenUnlockedThisDeviceOnly protection — they cannot be extracted by other apps or backed up to iCloud. Audio files are stored in the app's private sandbox, inaccessible to other apps.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when changes were made. Continued use of PAULA after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
